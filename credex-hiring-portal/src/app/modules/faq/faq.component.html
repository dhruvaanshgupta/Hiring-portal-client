FAQs

You can access the examination portal by completing the registration by filling essentials details and uploading resume after which you will gain access to give the examination.

You can register for an exam by navigating to the "Examination" page and completing the registration process.

If you have a question or concern about your exam or results, you can contact the administrator by sending an email to the provided email address or by using the "Contact Us" form on the website.

Exam results are calculated based on the grading criteria for each exam and the answers provided by the student. Exam results are usually reported in the form of grades, scores, or certificates, depending on the institution and the exam.
